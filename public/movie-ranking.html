The Greatest Movies Of the Year According To Aneesha

My Top Three Movie Choices

Magic Mike Last Dance

Mike takes the stage again, following a business deal that went bust, leaving him broke and taking bartender gigs in Florida. Mike heads to London with a wealthy socilite who lures him with an offer he cant refuse.

Ponniyin Selvan II

Arulmozhi Varman continues on his journey to become Rajaraja I, the greatest ruler of the historic Chola empire of south India.

Adipurush

A modern adaptation of the Indian epic Ramayana which follows the exiled prince Raghav's journey to rescue his wife Janaki from the demon king Lankesh.
